Crime by Offence

Bar Chart



The dataset captured several different types of theft includeing burglarly, robbery, theft featuring an automobile and theft other. The theft-related crime dominated the statistics. By contrast, other crimes such as sexual abuse, homicide and arson yields drastically lower volumes.

Taking a Geographic Perspective



Location data was logged with each incidence of crime. Although the Google Map heatmap allows the reader to quickly identify areas with high areas of crime, this visualization consider the entire year as a unit.

Using the Kepler plug-in, we are able to see these geogrpahic variations over smaller periods.
