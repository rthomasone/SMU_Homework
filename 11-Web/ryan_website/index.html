Washington Crime Data 2017

Brief synopsis of crime patterns in Washington DC during 2017.

Overview



The objective of the project was to analyze publicly available crime statistics for Washington DC during 2017. Specifically, a review of the distribution of crime over the city center and any trends related to various types of offences (e.g. burglary, assault) or weapons used to commit these crimes.
