Crime over Time

Analysis of Incidence of Crime over 2017

Previous Next


The carousel above cycles through four graphs illustrating the volume of crime over different periods. Taking the year in it entirety, we take a look at the number incidents over the:

  • 24-hour
  • Days in the Month
  • Days in the Week
  • Months in the year

The largest variation was seen in the hourly analysis. 12pm and 3pm emerge as peak times. In contrast, the lowest volumes of crime took place between midnight and 5am.

As the data was aggregated over larger periods, the differences were subtle. The number of incidents remained relatively consistent over the week. Reviewing by Day of Month, the 31st yielded a significant drop. However, this easily explained by a subset of months having 31 days.

With respect to month, January and May displayed the highest incidences. Conversely, March and December yielded the lowest numbers.
